How should collections fit into the home screen?

Your home screen currently shows rows of content from each user's installed Stremio addons.

A

Replace addon rows

Collections become the entire home screen. Users see only what you (the admin) have curated as folder tiles. No raw addon rows visible.

B

Layer on top

Collection rows are pinned above the existing addon catalog rows. Users get both — your curated folders at the top, then their addon content below.

C

Separate page

Collections live on their own page (e.g. /collections), completely separate from the home screen. Home screen stays as-is.
